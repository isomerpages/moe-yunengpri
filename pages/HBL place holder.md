<p>## Home Based Learning</p>
<p>To better equip our students with the skills necessary for independent
learning and to prepare them for potential future disruptions, all students
will participate in our E-Learning Day on&amp;nbsp;**Monday, 26 May 2025**.&lt;br&gt;</p>
<p>Through this E-Learning experience, students will:</p>
<p>a)&amp;nbsp;&amp;nbsp;&amp;nbsp; Enhance Readiness for E-Learning: By
practicing home-based learning, students will be better prepared to adapt
to prolonged school closures, while helping the school address any logistical
or technological challenges.</p>
<p>b)&amp;nbsp;&amp;nbsp;&amp;nbsp; Cultivate Self-Directed Learning Skills:
Students will learn to manage their time effectively, take ownership of
their learning, and seek assistance when needed within a digital environment.</p>
<p>c)&amp;nbsp;&amp;nbsp;&amp;nbsp;&amp;nbsp; Appreciate the Role of Technology
in Learning: Students will experience first-hand how technology can support
and enrich learning, collaboration, and communication beyond the traditional
classroom. &lt;br&gt;</p>
<p>Teachers will monitor students' learning progress and provide the necessary
support i.e. providing feedback through the online platforms. &lt;br&gt;</p>
